# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- Price: $29 per month
- Video limit: 10 videos per month
- Resolution: 720p
- AI Captions: Not included
- Support: Email support during business hours only

### Pro Plan
- Price: $79 per month
- Video limit: Unlimited videos
- Resolution: 4K
- AI Captions: Included
- Support: 24/7 live support included

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- Refunds are available within the first 7 days of purchase only
- No refunds are issued after 7 days under any circumstance
- Refund requests must be submitted via support email

### Support Policy
- 24/7 support is exclusively available to Pro plan subscribers
- Basic plan users receive email-only support during business hours (9am-6pm EST)

### General
- Both plans include access to the AutoStream web dashboard
- Users can upgrade from Basic to Pro at any time with prorated billing
- Annual billing offers a 20% discount on both plans
